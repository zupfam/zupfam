#### ZupFam — Hyperlocal Ad Network

**Core Direction:**  
Hyperlocal, economical, reliable ad network for small, high-footfall businesses.  
Focus: Pharmacies + Grocery Stores as primary launch verticals, with potential expansion to coffee shops, gyms, salons.

**Publisher Verticals**

1. Pharmacies → Doctors, diagnostic labs, supplement brands.
2. Grocery Stores → Restaurants, FMCG brands, local services.
3. Coffee Shops (secondary pilot) → Lifestyle brands, local events, nearby restaurants.
4. Gyms → Nutrient supplements, physiotherapists, sportswear brands.

**Publisher Requirements**

- Must have a display screen (Smart TV or TV + media stick)
- Must have Wi-Fi connection
- Preferably in high footfall locations with repeat customers.

**Revenue Model**

- **Phase 1 (First 15–20 locations):**
  - Flat payout: ₹1,000–₹2,000 per month to location owner.
  - Free in-store ad slots for their own offers.
  - No profit share initially to reduce complexity.
- **Phase 2 (Scale past 20 locations):**
  - Profit share model:
    - Publisher: 25% of ad revenue from their screen.
    - ZupFam: 75% (covers tech, sales, ops, content).

**Execution Plan (August 15–31)**

1. MVP Build
   - Simple ad scheduler + rotation.
   - Cloud CMS for uploading ads per location.
   - Device app (Android TV / Firestick / Raspberry Pi).
2. Publisher Onboarding
   - Target 5 pharmacies + 5 grocery stores in same neighborhoods.
   - Add 1–2 coffee shops for pilot testing.
3. Advertiser Acquisition
   - Phase 1: Doctors, FMCG distributors, restaurants, gyms.
   - Use same advertiser across multiple verticals.
4. Measurement of Success
   - Publisher count: # of active locations with live ads.
   - Advertiser count: # of paying advertisers per month.
   - Fill rate: % of available ad slots sold.
   - MRR: Monthly recurring revenue from advertisers.

**Long-term Vision**

- Multi-vertical, location-based ad network.
- Unified tech stack serving pharmacies, groceries, gyms, salons, coffee shops.
- Local businesses advertise where their customers already shop and hang out.
